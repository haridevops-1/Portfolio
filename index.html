My Journey Into Tech

From electronics student to aspiring frontend developer.

I come from Chennai, and my journey into tech started with a Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Central Polytechnic College, Tharamani. From the beginning, I was always curious to learn new technologies and explore different fields.

During my college time, I completed internships in different places and slowly developed an interest in cloud computing. To support my earnings, I worked as a Zomato delivery partner for more than one year and did part-time jobs. I earned and saved money to complete my AWS DevOps course successfully.

After finishing the course, I started searching for jobs, but I faced many rejections. I was rejected by more than 20 companies. However, I did not give up. Later, I heard about Freshworks STS Software Academy. I prepared seriously, attended the examination and the interview, and got selected among 2000+ students.

That moment became a turning point in my life. Now, at the academy, I am gaining strong technical skills, real-world project experience, and professional knowledge. I am also building my own projects to improve continuously. This is my journey so far, and I am working hard to grow further in my career.
